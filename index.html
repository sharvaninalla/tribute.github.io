One Direction

The boys who got put together in 10 minutes ruled a decade

One Direction through the years:

  • 2010 - It all started in 2010. The group was put together by Simon Cowell on July 23, 2010, after they all auditioned on The X Factor as solo artists.
  • 2011 - The group headed out on the X Factor Live Tour, where they performed for 500,000 people throughout the U.K. They then headed to Los Angeles to record their debut album, Up All Night.
  • 2012 - In March 2012, Up All Night came out in America, and the guys were inducted into the Guinness World Records after they became the first U.K. group to have their debut album reach number one in the U.S.
  • 2013 - Then, in July 2013, One Direction released “Best Song Ever” — the lead single off of their then-upcoming third studio album, Midnight Memories. The song is their highest charting single in the U.S. to date, reaching number two on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
  • 2014 - Naturally, fans were living when the band released another new song, called “Steal My Girl,” in September 2014. Two months later, in November, their fourth album, Four, hit stands. It sold 3.2 million copies and debuted at number one in 18 countries. One Direction then became the only group in the 58-year history of the Billboard 200 Albums chart to have their first four albums debut at number one.
  • 2015 - In February 2015, the guys embarked on their fourth world tour, the On the Road Again Tour. But fans were left shocked and devastated when Zayn announced he would be leaving the band in the middle of the tour.The guys continued their tour without Zayn. It concluded on October 31, 2015, and grossed $208 million.
